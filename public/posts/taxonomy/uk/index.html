The English Mosquito
Published on February 12th, 2008
There is a relatively new device called "The English Mosquito" which is being used in England to control where young people typically gather. The device works by emitting high-frequency sounds that only young people (typically younger than 20 years of age) have the ability to hear. Because it's fairly annoying, it causes young people to eventually leave a particular area.
